1. Introduction

Route 25, operated by rakTech LLC (“Route 25”, “we”, “us”, or “our”), is a Pokémon TCG-focused social network that lets users log pulls, track collections, and facilitate trades (“Service”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website and app.

By using Route 25, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Route 25 is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with The Pokémon Company, Nintendo, Game Freak, or Creatures Inc.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information you provide directly, such as:

  • Contact information (e.g., email address) when you join the waitlist or contact us.
  • Account information when you create or sign in to an account, such as display name, avatar, and login provider details (e.g., “Sign in with Apple” or Google).
  • Collection and trade data, including logged pulls, card details, trade offers, and trade history.
  • Images you upload, such as photos of cards for documentation or trade purposes.
  • Any messages or content you submit through the app.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the Service, we and our service providers may automatically collect certain information, such as:

  • Device information (for example, device model, operating system version, language, and app version).
  • Usage information (such as screens viewed, features used, and interactions within the app).
  • Approximate location information, if enabled at the device or app level.
  • Log data in the event of errors or crashes, including technical details to help diagnose issues.

2.3 Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to remember preferences, understand traffic, or support features such as analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain website features.

3. How We Use Information

We may use the information we collect for purposes such as:

  • Providing, operating, and improving the Service.

  • Sending you important notifications, such as changes to the Service, updates, or security alerts.
  • Sending you emails related to the waitlist, launch updates, and relevant news about Route 25, when you have provided your email for those purposes.
